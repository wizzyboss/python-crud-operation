About Us

Two Decades of
Sartorial Excellence

Agbodo Shalom Komlavi founded this atelier in 2010 with a simple mission: to create garments that make people feel confident, distinguished, and truly themselves. What began as a small tailoring shop in Lagos has grown into a trusted name in bespoke fashion.

Every garment that leaves our workshop carries the mark of meticulous attention — from the initial measurement to the final stitch. We blend traditional African textiles like Ankara, Aso-Oke, and George with contemporary silhouettes to create pieces that honor heritage while embracing modern style.
